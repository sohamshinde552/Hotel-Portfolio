Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out are available upon request, subject to availability.

Do you offer airport transportation?

Yes, we provide luxury airport transfer services. Please contact our concierge at least 24 hours in advance to arrange pickup.

Is parking available?

We offer both valet and self-parking options. Valet parking is complimentary for all guests, and self-parking is available in our underground garage.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, we are a pet-friendly hotel. We welcome dogs and cats up to 50 lbs. Please inform us during booking so we can prepare welcome amenities for your pet.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours or more before check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a one-night charge.

Do you have accessible rooms?

Yes, we offer ADA-compliant accessible rooms with roll-in showers, grab bars, and other accessibility features. Please request during booking.
